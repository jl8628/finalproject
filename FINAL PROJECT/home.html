QUALITY EDUCATION

ENSURE INCLUSIVE AND EQUITABLE QUALITY EDUCATION AND PROMOTE LIFELONG LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES

Quality Education is part of one of the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
It aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learnings opportunities for all. This goal recognizes education as a fundamental human right and a key driver for sustainable development. It emphasizes the importance of providing access to education, improving its quality, and fostering lifelong learning opportunities for people of all ages.
